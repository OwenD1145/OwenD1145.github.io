About Me

Throughout my career in the trades I've had the opportunity to interpret construciton documentation and bring that vision to reality. As a journeyman glazier I've demonstrated my understanding of structural/architectural details through quality installation. As an electrician apprentice I was taught codes and proper installation procedures for residential and commercial applications. As a site foreman I have coordinated with sub-contractors to ensure quality installation and have caught errors in both architectural and structural details.

I am currently obtaining certification for Revit Architecture 2023 at Front Range Community College. I also intend to get certification in AutoCAD as I am completing the preperation coursework supplied by Autodesk. I already have a solid foundational skillset in AutoCAD.

I am proficient at the following in Revit 2023:General Architecture, Floor Plans, Details, Elevations, 3D Rendering, 3D Walkthroughs, Structure, Massing and Site, Component Modeling, and some MEP. I am familiar with Dynamo and look for ways to increase my productivity.

Some of my related hobbies include CAD and 3D Printing which I've dabbled in since late 2015. When I'm not focused on design I like to spend time in nature hiking Colorado's many trails. I enjoy learning new things and I'm always looking to challenge myself. I'm a self-taught musician of 10 years, I understand the importance of practice!
